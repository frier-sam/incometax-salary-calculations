Income tax and salary calculation - INDIA

Annual Income Vs Income Tax

CALCULATION : Total Tax = Tax based on Taxable Income + Cess + Surcharge
  • Taxable Income : Total salary(salary + bonuses + variable) + External Income - 50,000INR(standard deductions)
  • Tax : Tax calculated using new regime
  • Cess : 4% of income tax
  • Surcharge : Surcharges on income tax if taxable income exceeds 50lakhs per annum

CTC vs Monthly Inhand Salary

CALCULATION : Monthly Inhand Salary = CTC - Provident Fund - Income Tax / 12
  • CTC : Cost To Company
  • Provident Fund : 10 - 12% of Basic Component (Taken as 11%)
  • Basic Component : Generally 40 - 50% of CTC (Taken as 50%)
  • Income Tax : Tax Calculated Using above method
